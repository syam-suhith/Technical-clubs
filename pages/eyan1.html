ABOUT

The E-Yantra Club is an initiative of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, aimed at promoting robotics education and fostering innovation in the field of robotics. The club is supported by the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India, and is open to students from all over India.

The E-Yantra Club also provides support and mentorship to students who are interested in pursuing careers in robotics and related fields. Members of the club have access to a wide network of professionals in the industry, as well as job opportunities, internships, and research projects.

Importance

The primary objective of the E-Yantra Club is to provide a platform for students to learn about robotics, develop their skills, and participate in various robotics competitions and challenges. The club offers a range of training programs and workshops, both online and offline, which are designed to help students gain hands-on experience with robotics and learn about the latest technologies and trends in the field.

In addition to training programs and workshops, the E-Yantra Club also organizes various robotics competitions and challenges throughout the year. These competitions are open to all members of the club and provide an opportunity for students to showcase their skills, creativity, and innovative ideas in robotics.
